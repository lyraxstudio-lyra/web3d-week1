Did you practice?

I made a practice room filled with violins, chairs, and chains.
Violin, my childhood dream, the torture, the source of my pride and my shame. Playing the violin for 20 years, practicing has always given me a feeling of torture and tiredness, but also satisfaction and achievement. The chains in the scene represent the invisible pressure that I experienced. In the context of violin playing, was the expectation that my mom, my teacher, had placed on me. Those expectations made me believe that the point of playing the violin was to be exceptional and accomplished.
Now, I have finally been able to enjoy the process of practicing just for the sake of playing.
